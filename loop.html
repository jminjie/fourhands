How to use looper

  1. Each player gets their own looper. Each looper can only have one layer
  2. Press "Record loop" to start recording your loop. Will automatically set the looper instrument to your current sampler.
  3. Press "End record " to finish recording the loop
  4. Press "Play/Pause" to play or pause the loop
  5. To record a new loop press "Record loop" again
